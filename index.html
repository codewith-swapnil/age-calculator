About Our Age Calculator

Our free age calculator tool helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth to today's date. It's perfect for:

  • Finding out how many days until your next birthday
  • Calculating age for official documents
  • Determining school or retirement eligibility
  • Tracking developmental milestones

The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to provide accurate results.
